Attack on Titan

Unless you've been living under a rock, or just have better things to do with your life, then you may know Attack on Titan as one of the most popular anime. And, that is for good reason. Attack on Titan sells a world quite unlike any else with an engrossing story and captivating high-quality action sequences.

Picture this now, Humanity was brought to the brink of extinction by monstrously large humanoid predators known as Titans. As a result, humanity created enormous concentric walls to take refugee behind against the threat of the titans. However, certain events transpire and one of the walls is breached by two new threats: the Armored Titan & Collosal Titan. As a result, humanity is further pushed back towards extinction.
