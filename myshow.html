Synopsis

Pacific Rim before Pacific Rim was Pacific Rim

In the year 2000, a global catacylsm by the name of "Second Impact" has devastated humanity. Taking place in the year 2015, the organization NERV's mission is locate and destroy foreign entities called "angels". To accomplish this, 14 year old children are recruited as child soldiers to fight back this terror and save Earth from a predicted "Third Impact".

Sounds depressing, doesn't it? However, don't fret, the 'h' in Neon Genesis Evangelion stands for happiness. Without debate, the greatest anime of the last millenia. To top it off, it even has a memorable title song. In some circles, it is even considered a sin to skip the title song.
